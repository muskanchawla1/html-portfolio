Spicy Guava Mocktail Recipe

Ingredients

For Preparation:

  • Cocktail shaker
  • Cocktail stirring spoon
  • 2 rocks glasses
  • 2 small plates or saucers
  • Ice, for shaker and for glass

For the fillings:

  • 3 ounces Torani Guava Syrup
  • 1 ounce freshly squeezed lime juice, plus more for rim
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder

Instructions

  1. Chilled sparkling mineral water
  2. Chile-lime seasoning (such as Tajin), for rim
  3. 2 small lime wheels, for garnish
